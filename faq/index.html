  • Basics

  • How do I get a LiraPay card?

    In order to get a LiraPay card, download our app and follow the provided guidelines. Once your account is activated, the rest comes easy. From the "Cards" menu you can choose to order a physical or a virtual card, or both.

  • What is the LiraPay virtual card?

    Your LiraPay virtual card becomes available in your account once your profile is activated, our KYC procedure is complete and you choose your currency. This card can be used as an additional safety measure, helping you avoid using the details on your physical card when completing a payment on a website with uncertain security. However, a virtual card cannot be used for ATM withdrawals or for POS payments in physical stores.

  • How do I activate my physical card?

    For your card to become permanently active, you need to make a purchase, irrespective of the amount, in any physical store, using your PIN code.

  • Do I have to pay anything when opening my account?

    Opening a LiraPay account is completely free of charge. Maybe a smile.

  • Top-Up

  • Bank Transfer

    When opening an account with LiraPay, you will receive a unique user code, which can be found in your account details section. In order to transfer money from another card to your LiraPay account, this unique used code will be required.
    As such, you can make transfers from any bank account to the LiraPay general IBAN, having this user code mentioned in the "payment details" box.

  • Transfers from another LiraPay account

    To accept transfers from another LiraPay account, you have two options:
    ‚óè Provide the sender with your unique code;
    ‚óè Use the phone number you provided when creating your account with us.

  • Top-ups using another personal card

    You can load your account using another personal card by using the Top-up option. The loading taxes are the ones charged by the issuing bank of the card you want to use to top-up your LiraPay account.

  • Account

  • Can I receive my salary in my LiraPay account?

    For the time being, this option is unavailable. However, we are working on providing this option as well and we aim at doing so as soon as possible. If you'd like to be notified when this option becomes available, we invite you to leave your email address below and we will let you know.

  • How long does it take for money to reach my account?

    When making transfers from another LiraPay account, money reaches your account in a few minutes. On the other hand, the bank generally processes transfers the next working day, therefore the money will reach your account within 2 to 3 working days. Please keep in mind that your LiraPay unique user code is mandatory for transfers. Unless you mention it, we are unable to accept transfers and the money will return to the bank account you used for the transaction.

  • What is the currency I can use for (physical or virtual) card payments?

    You can use your LiraPay card for payments in any country and any currency. The conversion will be automatically processed according to the VISA exchange rate.

  • How can I make online card payments?span>

    Paying online with LiraPay is similar to any other likewise process, using the 16 digit number, month and date of expiry, as well as the 3 digit security code (CVC). All information is printed on the front side of the card, along with the first and last name of the card holder. For enhanced security, for each online payment you will be sent a text message on your mobile phone with a unique verification code.

  • Payments

  • Cash withdrawal

    You can withdraw cash from any ATM accepting VISA cards, in any country.

  • I forgot my password

    To reset your password you need to open the LiraPay app, click on "Forgot Password", enter your email address and your PIN code. You will receive a new password to your preferred contact option (email or mobile phone text message). When you first log in with the automatically generated password, you will be prompted to choose a new one.

  • WI changed my ID

    If you changed your ID and the data used when creating your LiraPay account no longer matches, please contact us.

  • Privacy

  • Closing my account

    LiraPay has no monthly administration fees or other account associated expenses, therefore, whether you use your account or not, there is no cost involved. Please consider your options thoroughly before closing your account because, should you wish to open a new account, since you are already in our database, the process takes longer and will be more complex. However, if you still want to close your account, this option is available in the app, in the Profile Settings tab. Please bear in mind that your data will not be erased from our database once your account is closed. According to regulations, we are compelled to keep it for any possible verifications regarding activities such as money laundering, terrorist actions, etc.

  • How do I know my personal data is protected?

    Our protocol instructs for thorough protection of your confidentiality and personal information. We ensure this protection by engaging with safeguarded third-party data centers with cutting-edge firewalls, complying with applicable laws in the field.

  • What are the accepted countries?

    For the moment, we can only take submissions from the legal citizens of Great Britain and in the EEA (European Economic Area): Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Republic of Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden.

  • Is there an age limit?

    Yes, LiraPay requires its prospective account users to be no younger than 18 years of age.

  • Delivery

  • Why should my identity be checked?

    A routine verification of the provided identification data is required for your LiraPay to become active and accessible to you. We are doing this to abide by the banking law certified as KYC (Know Your Client) or CDD (Customer Due Diligence). This process assists a company in confirming its clients' identity and avoiding corruption and extortion.

  • My identity confirmation failed

    Our verification procedures can take up to 24 hours (for exceptional cases, it could take even longer). Should you receive no reply from us within this time frame or if your identity confirmation failed, please retake the process and make sure that all pictures are clear (not obscure, nor glaring) and the writing is intelligible. To facilitate the process of identity confirmation, please insure that:
    ‚óè You are of age (18 years and above) and live in the UK or the European Economic Area;
    ‚óè Your ID is valid and the data on it is comprehensible.
    ! Your account cannot be verified with a screenshot, copy or scan of your ID !

  • I can no longer access my account

    Having unrestricted access to your account is critical and we are aware of this. As soon as we complete the verification procedures and if everything goes well you will regain access to your account and funds. However, we are unable to provide a feasible time span, as each situation is unique.

  • I can't use my physical card

    If you can't make transactions with your card, please run a few checkups:
    ‚óè The card was successfully activated;
    ‚óè Whether your card is frozen or not. It can happen to anyone, by accident. This information is available in the "Cards" tab, in the LiraPay app; if that's the case, click the "Unfreeze" button;
    ‚óè Review your security settings for not having particular types of transactions blocked. Click the 'PIN & Security' tab under the specified card.
    ‚óè For online transactions, check that the entered card details are correct (account holder name, card number, CVC, expiry date and PIN code).
    Should you block it as a result of three failed attempts, you have an option to unblock it.
    If you have a damaged or deteriorated physical card, you can easily replace it.
    Fees may apply. We will provide the expected delivery time and date at checkout.
