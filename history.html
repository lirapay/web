Our history

LIRAPAY is an adventure! Walk with us and let our cutting-edge mobile platform be your guide.

Our history has began in 1848 in Galatzi, a Romanian port on the Danube, where Zanni. M. Chrissoveloni, set up its first operating office under the name of ‘’Chrissoveloni Fils, maison d’exportation et de banque”.
In 1889 the business changes its name again to "N. Chrissoveloni, banker", and most importantly also changes its strategy by starting to invest and hence contribute significantly to the industrial development (not to call it revolution) of Romania.
In 1920 Jean N. Chrissoveloni as President and Founder of Banca Chrissoveloni S.A.R. starts an association with another Romanian Bank – Banca de Credit Roman; Jean N. Chrissoveloni also acquired a bank in Cluj - Banca Ardeleana și Casa de Economii and develops a multinational bank network with offices in Constantinople, Vienna, Paris and New York.
In 1928 the capital was increased to 350 million Lei by a group of foreign banks: Banque de Bruxelles, Comptoire d’Escompte de Geneve, Hambros Bank Ltd. London, W.A. Harriman and Co. New York, Banque Transatlantique Paris, Nieder Osterreichische Escompte-Gesselschalt Vienna.
In 1948 the Bank was confiscated and liquidated by the new Government.
In 1999 John Chrissoveloni is founding PayNet srl in Bucharest, together with a Greek Investment Fund. It was the first Third Party Processor in Romania, authorized by VISA and MasterCard for personalizing cards, issuing and acquiring card transactions.
PayNet was a pioneer for cards industry in Romania, being one of the promoters of new products and technologies in the market (e.g. First installment card, first affinity card issued in partnership with a Trade Union).
For almost a decade, PayNet served as processor for 25 small and medium Banks from Romania, Serbia, Montenegro and Cyprus and in 2011 was integrated into one of the biggest worldwide processors.
In 2018, based on our experience in cards industry, we launch LIRAPAY, a mobile payment facility based on a prepaid VISA card. The company was incorporated in Malta by Mr. John Chrissoveloni and is offering digital financial services to entire EEA area.
